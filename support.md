## Getting started

**Do I need an account?** No. IGNITE works fully offline and stores everything on your device. You only need an account if you want **Cloud sync** across devices (a Pro feature).

**How do I add my car?** Open the Garage tab and tap add. Enter your registration and we'll look up the make, model, fuel type and MOT history via DVLA/DVSA, or enter the details manually.

**How do I log a refuel?** Tap the **+** button and choose *Refuel*. Enter litres, price and your odometer reading — IGNITE calculates cost and estimated MPG live.

## Accounts & sign-in

**How do I sign in?** Settings → Account. You can use email + password or **Sign in with Google**.

**I forgot my password.** On the sign-in screen, tap *Forgot password?* and follow the email link to set a new one. (Not applicable if you use Google sign-in.)

**How do I delete my account?** See [Delete your account]({{ '/delete-account/' | relative_url }}). You can do it in-app in one tap, or request it by email.

## Subscriptions

**What's free?** Logging refuels, expenses and services, plus basic MPG and cost-per-mile stats, one vehicle, 6 months of history, up to 3 reminders, and CSV import/export — free forever, no ads.

**What does Pro add?** Cloud sync, unlimited vehicles, full history, unlimited MOT/tax/service reminders, and advanced analytics. £2.99/month or £19.99/year.

**How do I cancel?** Subscriptions are billed by Apple or Google. Manage or cancel in your **App Store** or **Google Play** account subscription settings — the in-app Account screen links you straight there. Cancelling keeps Pro active until the end of the period you've paid for.

**I paid but Pro isn't showing.** Open Settings → Account and tap *Restore purchases*. If it still doesn't appear, email us with your store receipt.

## Your data

**Where is my data stored?** On your device by default. With Cloud sync on, a copy is mirrored to our backend, encrypted in transit and isolated to your account. See the [Privacy Policy]({{ '/privacy/' | relative_url }}).

**Can I export my data?** Yes — History → export gives you a CSV of your records. Free on every plan.
